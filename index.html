Student Life

At Hayatul Islamic School, we believe education extends beyond the classroom. Our vibrant student life program offers a balanced approach to academic, spiritual, and personal development.

Students enjoy a structured daily routine that includes academic classes, Quran memorization sessions, sports activities, and creative arts programs. Our boarding facilities provide a home-like environment with modern amenities.

We organize regular extracurricular activities including debates, science fairs, Islamic competitions, and community service projects to develop well-rounded individuals.

Our students benefit from mentorship programs, career guidance sessions, and leadership opportunities that prepare them for life beyond school.
